About the Project

MMMM is an innovative framework that combines the traditional appeal of magazines with modern interactive mapping and rich metadata. Our platform allows readers to explore content through geographic locations, discovering how stories connect across space and time.

Each issue focuses on specific themes, presenting carefully curated articles that are enhanced with geographic references and interconnected through a sophisticated metadata layer. Navigate through our collections using interactive maps, explore relationships between articles, and discover new perspectives on fascinating topics.
